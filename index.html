-- ABOUT --

QUIBBLE RACE WAS ORIGINALLY DEVELOPED BY JON
PERRY AND DEREK YU IN 1998 UNDER THE LABEL
BLACKEYE SOFTWARE. IN 2004, THE GAME WAS REMADE
BY DEREK YU IN THE FORM OF HIS FIRST FLASH
GAME. OVER TWENTY YEARS LATER, QUIBBLE RACE WAS
RE-RELEASED WITHIN THE RETRO INSPIRED GAME
COLLECTION UFO 50. JOINED BY EIRIK SUHRKE, PAUL
HUBANS, TYRIQ PLUMMER, AND OJIRO FUMOTO, PERRY
AND YU'S FINAL ADAPTATION KEPT MUCH OF THE
CHARM OF THE ORIGINAL.

WHAT YOU SEE ABOVE IS YET ANOTHER REMAKE.
WRITTEN IN C++, AND COMPILED TO WEBASSEMBLY
WITH EMSCRIPTEN, THIS VERSION IS AN UNOFFICIAL
REMAKE WHICH ADDS ONLINE FUNCTIONALITY. THIS IS
A FREE, FAN-MADE CLONE, MADE WITHOUT OWNERSHIP
OVER THE CONCEPT, MECHANICS, OR ARTWORK. ALL
ASSETS, MUSIC, AND CHARACTERS BELONG TO DEREK
YU'S STUDIO, MOSSMOUTH GAMES.

IF YOU ENJOYED THIS VERSION OF QUIBBLE RACE,
PLEASE GO CHECK OUT UFO 50. QUIBBLE RACE IS
ONLY ONE SMALL PART OF THE 50 GAMES AVAILABLE
WITHIN THE COLLECTION, ALL OF WHICH ARE AS
INCREDIBLE AS THIS ONE.
