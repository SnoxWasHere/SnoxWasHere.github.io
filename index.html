-- ABOUT --

QUIBBLE RACE WAS ORIGINALLY DEVELOPED BY JON PERRY AND DEREK YU IN 1998 UNDER THE LABEL BLACKEYE SOFTWARE. IN 2004, THE GAME WAS REMADE BY DEREK YU INTO HIS FIRST FLASH GAME. OVER TWENTY YEARS LATER, QUIBBLE RACE WAS RE-RELEASED WITHIN THE RETRO INSPIRED GAME COLLECTION UFO 50. JOINED BY EIRIK SUHRKE, PAUL HUBANS, TYRIQ PLUMMER, AND OJIRO FUMOTO, PERRY AND YU'S FINAL ADAPTATION WAS OVER TWO DECADES IN THE MAKING.

WHAT YOU SEE ABOVE IS YET ANOTHER REMAKE. REWRITTEN IN C++, AND COMPILED TO WEBASSEMBLY WITH EMSCRIPTEN, THIS VERSION IS AN UNOFFICIAL REMAKE WHICH ADDS ONLINE AND MOBILE FUNCTIONALITY. THIS IS A FREE, FAN-MADE CLONE, MADE WITHOUT OWNERSHIP OVER THE CONCEPT, MECHANICS, OR ARTWORK. ALL ASSETS, MUSIC, AND CHARACTERS BELONG TO DEREK YU'S STUDIO, MOSSMOUTH GAMES.

IF YOU ENJOYED THIS VERSION OF QUIBBLE RACE, PLEASE GO CHECK OUT UFO 50. QUIBBLE RACE IS ONLY ONE SMALL PART OF THE 50 GAMES AVAILABLE WITHIN THE COLLECTION, EACH OF WHICH ARE AS INCREDIBLE AS THIS ONE.
